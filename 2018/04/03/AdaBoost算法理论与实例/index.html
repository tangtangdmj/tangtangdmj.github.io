Boosting

一般的Boosting过程

Boosting是一族可将弱学习器提升为强学习器的算法。
Boosting源于1989年Kearns and Valiant[1]提出的理论问题：“弱学习是否等价于强学习？”。
输入：样本分布：D\mathcal{D}D

[1]: Kearns M.J., Valiant L.G. (1993) Cryptographic limitations on learning Boolean formulae and finite automata. In: Hanson S.J., Remmele W., Rivest R.L. (eds) Machine Learning: From Theory to Applications. Lecture Notes in Computer Science, vol 661. Springer, Berlin, Heidelberg
